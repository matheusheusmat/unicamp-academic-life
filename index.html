My name is Matheus and I am a student at Universidade Estadual de Campinas - Unicamp. It is a public university located in the city of Campinas, state of SÃ£o Paulo, Brazil. I would like to share to you some of my experiences of living and studying at this institution, and some facts, too.

The Universidade Estadual de Campinas is a great place to live, socialize, learn and grow academically, professionally and as a person. This is the life I chose to myself, although it is difficult sometimes. But, what keeps me going on is the faith in the reward. A successful carrer won't start without time and hard work. Each day I pass there, I discover something new. My interests are processor architechtures, scientific programming and artificial intelligence.
